    Cybersecurity 101



    What is Cybersecurity Important?:

    Cybersecurity is an ongoing effort to help protect the information of the world from potental cyberattacks. Its not about what will happen but when..It is our job to stay vigilant and protect our networking systems and data from unauthorized use or harm.



    Click on the cards to find out more about the three levels of protection



    Personal

    It's You! Your personal data, indentity and any other information that defines your user. It is important to safegaurd this data!

    Organizational

    As an organization, it is important that all members take responsibility to protect and uphold an organization's reputation, senstive data and relationships

    Governments

    At this level it becomes even more cruitial to protect data as this could be a threat to national security, economic stability and the saftey of those governed.



    Your Data:

    Personal data is any information about you that can help identify you both online and offline.



    Online

    Information that can be viewed on electronic devices and sometimes stored in the cloud such as:



    • Username
    • Email
    • Virtual Persona
    • Documents
    • Social Media


    Offline

    Information that is about your personal day to day life such as:



    • Name
    • Address
    • Age
    • Gender
    • Physical Features


    It is important for both types of data to be secure as this can easily be accessed and taken from under you at anytime!
